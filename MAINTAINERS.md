## What maintainers do

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage issues and security reports
- Make architectural decisions
- Cut releases
- Represent the project in the community

## Becoming a maintainer

We follow a simple path: start by contributing, then take on reviewer responsibilities, and eventually join as a maintainer. See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for details.
